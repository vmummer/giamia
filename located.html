GiaMia Villas

Campo di Fano is a village and belongs to the municipality of Prezza, in the province of L`Aquila, in the region Abruzzo. Its closet neighbor is the village of Torre dei Nolfi and approx 5 min drive to the City of Sulmona.

Directions:

Arriving by Airplane:

Campo di Fano is situated in the middle of Abruzzo and serviced by three Airports:

  • Abruzzo International Airport (PSR) - Pescara, Italy
  • Leonardo da Vinci - Fiumicino (FCO) Airport - Rome, Italy
  • Rome Ciampino Airport - (CIA) - Rome, Italy

Arriving by Train:

  • Sulmona Train Station

Arriving by Bus:

  • Sulmona Bus Station
