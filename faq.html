GiaMia Villas



  1. When will we be opened?
    A: We are almost finished construction and will be ready for Spring 2009

  2. What is the rate for the Villas?
    A: Are current rates are posted on the web site here.

  3. Is there Internet Access?
    A: The plan is to have a Internet Connect in each room. There will also be WiFi access.

  4. Can I cook in the Villa?
    A: Each Apartment has its own Kitchen, including an Electric Stove, Refrigerator, Cutlery, Dishes, Pots and Pans

  5. Is there somewhere to do Laundry?
    A: Currently there are no onsite Laundry facilities. Each room do have Irons and Ironing Boards.

  6. How many people can sleep in Villas?
    A: The Villas are designed to accommodate two adults and some suites have a pull out sofa's to accommodate another two more children.

  7. Is there food included?
    A: There is no food supplied, but there are place to shop in Sulmona and Pratola approx 5km away
